How it works?

Post a Job

Add your job opening to our free job board. Include the job description (JD) or paste the JD link.

Create AI-Powered Test

Our AI analyzes your job description to generate an objective test. Customize test settings like number of questions, time limit and passing score.

Instant Results & Analytics

Candidates take the test immediately upon applying, streamlining the hiring process. Test results and detailed analytics are available in real-time on your dashboard.
